Desert Sonic

One day, Sonic decided that he wanted to go for a run through the grassy plains of Green Hill Zone. He decided that he wanted to explore the ENTIRETY of it, which will take roughly 8 hours to do. Sonic is also impatient, so if you are not going RIGHT or moving at all, he will use his godlike powers to reset the game. At least you have those good old Green Hill Zone tunes to listen to throughout it. Can you make it through the entire 8 hours?

This is a rom hack parody of an unreleased game for the Sega CD titled Desert Bus.
